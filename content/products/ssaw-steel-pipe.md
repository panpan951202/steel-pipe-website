Spiral Submerged Arc Welded (SSAW) steel pipes are manufactured by continuously forming hot-rolled steel coils into a spiral shape and welding both the inside and outside using the submerged arc welding process. This unique spiral seam configuration distributes stress more evenly and allows for efficient production of very large diameter pipes up to 120 inches.

SSAW pipes are highly versatile and cost-effective, particularly for large-diameter fluid transportation, water supply systems, structural piling, and infrastructure projects. The spiral weld also provides natural resistance to crack propagation, making SSAW pipes a reliable choice for demanding environments.
